Nessus Finding Mapping

Translate Nessus scan findings into CIS Control maturity indicators. Use these alongside policy review, staff interviews, and third-party audit findings — not as a standalone score.

How to use: Search for a Nessus finding type or plugin category. Each card shows which CIS control is affected, the maturity implication, and a ready-made evidence statement to paste into the calculator's Nessus field.
⚠ Nessus is one evidence source. The Calculator also captures policy reviews, staff interviews, and third-party audit findings — all four sources together make a score defensible.
